Tracksend Plugin allows you to send timely and personalized messages to your Woocommerce store so you never lose out of a sale

== Description ==

Tracksend helps you send timely and personalized SMS by listening for and understanding cues from visitors, subscribers, and customers, and turning that information into valuable, relevant messages.

In fact, eCommerce stores integrating to Tracksend see an increase in ROI—that’s real growth driven by using SMS that has 4-6X open rates.

Join brands you love like Afrimash, House of Tara, and Small Chops growing faster & building amazing customer experiences 


**Never Lose Out On a Sale**

* Abandoned cart and check-out
Conversion reduces when a visitor or subscriber or customers abandoned cart for more than 1 hour. We solve this by listening to your Woocommerce store for a customer that abandons and gives you an option to send a timely message to bring that back to cart or another sales channel


* Payment Failed 
You don't have control over the payment gateway, sales fail at this point. Send an alternative payment method or a support line that allows you to support the customer

**Meaningful Customer Experience**

Stay connected with customer across the order journey with the following triggers

* Order Processing
* Order Shipped 
* Payment Refunded 
* Order on Hold

== Installation ==

Integrating Tracksend and your WooCommerce store is a quick, two-step process:

Install/activate Tracksend’s plugin.
Go to Integration under settings within your Tracksend account to enable the Woocommerce integration.
